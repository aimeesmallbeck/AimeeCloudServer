# AimeeCloud: The Interactive Layer for Robots

## Elevator Pitch

"AimeeCloud is the **AI-powered interactive layer for robots** — a SaaS platform that *automatically generates* games, education, and social features for any robot manufacturer.

While companies build great hardware, they struggle to create compelling *interactive experiences*. We handle it. Through intelligent agent workflows, manufacturers can request virtually any game or lesson plan, and AimeeCloud instantly generates the voice AI, robotics commands, and interactive content, tailored to their robot's specific capabilities. Robot manufacturers integrate via our API, and their robots instantly gain natural conversation, **AI-generated custom games**, educational content, and expressive personality — no wake word required.

**What makes us a unique investment:**
- **AI GENERATED EXPERIENCES ON DEMAND:** We don't just provide games; we *generate* them. Our agent workflows let users request virtually any game or lesson, and AimeeCloud auto-builds the complete robot interaction. This is the future of robot personalization.
- **Proven on our robots first** — Two live robots (UGV02 rover + arm) demonstrating the platform at OpenSauce July 17
- **Built for Scale** — Cloud-native architecture, tested to 50+ concurrent sessions, works with ANY robot through capability negotiation
- **Clear Business Model** — Free developer tier for hobbyists, paid tiers for manufacturers shipping products with unique, auto-generated experiences.
- **B2B Ready** — Per-robot API keys, rate limiting, enterprise auth options

We're submitting to the Hackster contest and demoing at OpenSauce to generate our first manufacturer conversations and show the power of AI-driven robot experiences."
